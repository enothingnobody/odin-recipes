THE BEST GARLIC BREAD!

This is the best garlic bread I have ever tasted. Adjust the ingredients as necessary until the mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese and garlic powder mixture is a paste-like consistency.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • Â½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 (1pound) loaf Italian bread

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven's boiler
  2. Mix together mayonnaise,Parmesan cheese, and garlic powder in a medium bowl until thick and well combined.
  3. Slice loaf in half lenghtwise and spread the cut-side of each piece with mayonnaise mixture. Place halves on a medium baking sheet.
  4. Cook under the preheated broiler until mayonaise mixture is bubbling and golden brown, 2 to 5 minutes; check frequently to avoid burning.
